Gravimetry

studies

By means of an agile and rapid reconnaissance survey, the magnitude of the variations we measure of the vertical component of gravity at a point is determined, for this a high accuracy instrument is necessary when measuring Milligales or even better microgal.

With the application of this method, reasonable geological models can be taken, especially in salt domes that are relatively complex. A contribution of this study methodology is critical for the development of the fields in the southeast of Mexico, improve the definition of deposits associated with this type of structures, reduce the risk in the incorporation of reserves and production increases.
